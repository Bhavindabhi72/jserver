Jetserver Service Features

We are up-to-date on the latest technologies and use them while emphasizing the issue of information security and maximum stability of the infrastructures.

Easy switching between PHP versions

Each server comes with a PHP management system and we support all versions of PHP (from PHP 5 to 8.3), you can switch versions independently with a few clicks from the admin panel.

Installing systems in a click!

Using the Softaculous installation system, you can easily install common systems such as WordPress, Joomla, Drupal and more from the management panel. We support installations of over 250 systems. The system also supports updates and notifications about new versions.

The best backup system in the world

Our JetBackup backup system is embedded in all servers. You can restore the account independently from the management panel with a few clicks. The recovery is for the entire account, for individual files, recovery of settings such as DNS, CRONS, SSL certificates, recovery for a single database. Also - performing a SnapShot independently for the account!

Domain registration

We provide private and business customers with a service of domain registration, domain renewal, domain transfer, domain name management and everything related to domain security. Registering a suitable and correct domain name will transmit to the site's target audience the message that the site wants to convey and will also create stimulation and attraction for the appropriate target audience for the site.

DEVOPS services

We at JetServer know the field well and provide DevOps services to customers whose website requirements require service beyond regular technical support.

We accompany development teams in the process of setting up the site, copying the site to the correct server structure, creating development environments through selective and secure automation and in fact everything the client, or anyone on their behalf, needs in the process.

Like every person who is a person, every team member is also different and we are sure that you will find a common denominator with one or more of us.

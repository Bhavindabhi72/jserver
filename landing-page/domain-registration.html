Why an Israeli domain?

Already in the first stages of the realization of the business initiative, the original service or the fresh business - it is recommended to create a unique internet identity, which will be an important branding basis for the unique presence of any enterprise or business, on and off the web. This means - reserve a suitable domain (or in Hebrew: domain name).

Registering a domain means creating an independent digital asset, which is the permanent internet address for a business or an idea. It can be used as the web address of an information site about the business, of its virtual store, used as a landing page, and of course - a business email address and a basis for referrals from social networks.

The domain allows the founders of the business to maintain an independent identity, and not to rely on platforms that can suddenly change rules, or stop operating.

Therefore - it is important to carry out the domain selection process in the best possible way.

About JetServer

Jetserver was established in 2006 with the goal of being a web hosting company that stands at the forefront of technology and providing first class service! We are veterans of the hi-tech world in Israel who have realized that fast, secure and stable web hosting combined with providing a humane, personal and uncompromising service is not common property. This is exactly what we decided to change. Jetserver has several server farms in Israel, Europe and the United States, with configuration and customization, and also around the world for the cloud services we offer. All this to provide a response to the needs of every customer in every location and every target audience.

The support system was founded on the human concept of loving people and helping others. Our support team consists of a variety of staff members with diverse technological backgrounds, such as: research and development, data communication, system, information security, advertising and marketing, design, project management, etc. We contribute a lot to the open source community on an international scale and provide support around the clock - 24/7 of quality and professional human response.

The services we offer are suitable for everyone, small and large, companies at all levels, business owners, developers, in fact who isn't?

On a personal note, your success is very important to us and we go far beyond the "acceptable" so that on the side of hosting the website(s) and/or application(s)

everything will be 110% fast, secure, stable and accessible without compromise.
